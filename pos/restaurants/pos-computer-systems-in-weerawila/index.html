Kitchen Order Ticket (KOT) System

The **Kitchen Order Ticket (KOT) system** is a game-changer for restaurants in Weerawila, ensuring smooth **order management and kitchen communication**. As part of our **advanced computer systems in Weerawila**, the KOT system eliminates handwritten orders, reducing errors, **enhancing workflow efficiency**, and **speeding up food preparation**.

How the KOT System Works

When a customer places an order—whether **dine-in, takeaway, or delivery**—the **POS system** instantly generates a **Kitchen Order Ticket (KOT)**. This ticket is automatically sent to the **kitchen display or printer**, listing the **food items, quantity, and special instructions**. Kitchen staff can **prioritize** and **prepare orders efficiently**, minimizing wait times and improving service.

Key Features of Our KOT System

  • ✅ **Instant Order Transmission** – Orders go directly to the kitchen, minimizing delays.
  • ✅ **Categorized Order Display** – Food, drinks, and special requests are automatically sorted.
  • ✅ **Real-Time Order Tracking** – Staff can monitor order status for timely preparation.
  • ✅ **Error-Free Orders** – Eliminates handwritten mistakes and miscommunication.
  • ✅ **Seamless POS Integration** – Ensures **accurate billing** and prevents unauthorized changes.
  • ✅ **Multi-Station Printing** – Directs orders to **different kitchen sections (main, bar, desserts, etc.)**.
