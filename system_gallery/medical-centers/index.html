Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our medical center management system

Is the system compliant with Sri Lankan healthcare regulations?
Yes, our system is fully compliant with Sri Lankan Ministry of Health guidelines and international healthcare standards including HIPAA. We ensure all patient data is encrypted and stored securely according to local regulations.
How long does it take to implement the system?
Most medical centers are fully operational within 48 hours. Our healthcare IT specialists handle data migration, system setup, and comprehensive staff training. We also provide dedicated support during the first week to ensure smooth operations.
Can the system integrate with existing laboratory equipment?
Yes, our system integrates with most modern laboratory equipment and diagnostic devices. We support HL7 standards and can create custom integrations for specialized equipment commonly used in Sri Lankan medical facilities.
What happens to our data if we decide to switch systems?
Your data belongs to you completely. We provide full data export capabilities in standard formats (CSV, XML, HL7) at any time. There are no data hostage situations - you can migrate to another system whenever you choose.
Is training provided for medical staff?
Absolutely! We provide comprehensive training for all medical and administrative staff. This includes on-site training sessions, video tutorials, user manuals, and ongoing support to ensure everyone is comfortable with the system.
Can patients access their records online?
Yes, our patient portal allows patients to view their medical records, book appointments, access lab results, and communicate securely with their healthcare providers. The portal is available in Sinhala, Tamil, and English for better accessibility.
