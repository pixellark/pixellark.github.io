Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mobile management system

How does IMEI tracking work and is it legal in Sri Lanka?
Yes, IMEI tracking for business inventory management is completely legal in Sri Lanka. Our system simply stores IMEI numbers for your own devices to prevent theft, manage warranties, and track device history. This is standard practice for mobile retailers and helps protect both your business and customers.
Can the system work without internet connection?
Yes! Our system has robust offline capabilities. You can continue sales, repairs, and basic operations even during internet outages. All data automatically syncs when connectivity returns, ensuring no loss of information or sales.
Does it integrate with existing accounting software?
Absolutely! Our system integrates with popular accounting software used in Sri Lanka including QuickBooks, Tally, and other standard accounting platforms. We also provide CSV/Excel exports for easy data transfer to any accounting system.
How does SMS integration work and what are the costs?
Our SMS integration works with major Sri Lankan providers (Dialog, Mobitel, Hutch, Airtel). Additional messages are charged at standard rates, typically LKR 0.30 per message or according to the package you select.
What kind of support do you provide?
We provide comprehensive support in Sinhala and English. This includes free installation, data migration, staff training, and 7 days of intensive support. Our team understands the Sri Lankan mobile market and provides ongoing assistance via phone, WhatsApp, and email.
Can we customize the system for our specific needs?
Yes! Our system is highly customizable. We can adapt the interface, reports, and workflows to match your business processes. Whether you're a small mobile shop or a large electronics retailer, we'll configure the system to work exactly how you need it.
How secure is customer data and business information?
Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted, regularly backed up, and stored securely. We comply with international data protection standards and ensure your customer information and business data are completely protected from unauthorized access.
