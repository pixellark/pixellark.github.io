Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pharmacy management system

How quickly can we implement the system in our pharmacy?
Most pharmacies are fully operational within 48 hours. Our implementation team handles data migration, staff training, and hardware setup. We also provide dedicated support during your first week to ensure seamless transition from your existing system.
Can we migrate data from our current pharmacy system?
Absolutely! We support data migration from over 50+ pharmacy systems including Pioneer, QS/1, PioneerRx, and others. Our migration specialists ensure 100% data integrity during the transfer, including patient profiles, prescription histories, and inventory data.
What kind of support do you provide?
We provide comprehensive support including 24/7 phone support for critical issues, live chat during business hours, video training sessions, and on-site visits when needed. Every pharmacy gets a dedicated account manager and access to our extensive knowledge base.
How secure is patient data in your system?
Patient data security is our top priority. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. All data is stored in cloud servers with automatic backups and disaster recovery protocols.
Can the system handle multiple pharmacy locations?
Yes! Our Enterprise solution supports unlimited locations with centralized management. You can track inventory, transfer prescriptions, and manage staff across all locations from a single dashboard. Each location can also operate independently if needed.
