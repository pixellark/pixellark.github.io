How quickly can we get the POS system up and running?
Most restaurants are fully operational within 24 hours. Our expert installation team handles hardware setup, data migration, and staff training. We also provide 24/7 support during your first week to ensure a smooth transition.
What happens if the internet goes down during service?
Our POS systems are capable of offline mode integrations! You can continue taking orders, processing payments, and running your restaurant. Once connectivity returns, all data automatically syncs to the cloud. Zero downtime, zero lost sales.
Can we integrate with our existing accounting software?
Absolutely! Our POS system integrates with 200+ popular accounting platforms including QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, and more. We also offer custom API integrations for enterprise clients with specific requirements.

Security is our top priority. We're PCI DSS Level 1 certified, use end-to-end encryption, and employ the same security standards as major banks. Your customer data is completely protected.

Yes! Our POS system is highly customizable. From menu layouts to reporting dashboards, we can tailor everything to match your restaurant's unique workflow and branding requirements.
