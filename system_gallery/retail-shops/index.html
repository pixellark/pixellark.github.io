Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our retail management system

What types of retail businesses can use this system?
Our system works for all types of retail businesses. It's fully customizable to match your specific business needs and industry requirements.
Do you provide barcode scanners and hardware?
Yes! We provide complete hardware packages including barcode scanners, receipt printers, cash drawers, and touch screen terminals. All hardware is sourced locally in Sri Lanka with full warranty and support.
Can I migrate my existing product data?
Absolutely! Our technical team will help you migrate all your existing product data, customer information, and transaction history. We support data import from Excel files, CSV formats, and most other POS systems.
How does the customer loyalty program work?
Our loyalty program automatically tracks customer purchases and awards points based on your custom rules. Customers can redeem points for discounts, and the system can send automated SMS messages for birthdays, promotions, and point balance updates.
What support do you provide after installation?
Every package includes free 24/7 phone and WhatsApp support for the first week. After that, you'll have ongoing support during business hours (8am-6pm, except Sundays) for 3 months.
Is the system compatible with Sri Lankan tax requirements?
Yes! Our system is fully compliant with Sri Lankan tax regulations including VAT calculations, NBT handling, and can generate all required tax reports. We stay updated with any changes in local tax laws to ensure your business remains compliant.
Can I manage multiple store locations?
Our Enterprise package supports unlimited locations with centralized management. All of these systems are fully intergrated with our ERP system for maximum experience across all connected assests.
