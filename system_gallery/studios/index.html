Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our studio management system

How quickly can we implement the system in our studio?
Most studios are fully operational within 48 hours. Our implementation team handles system setup, data migration from existing tools, and comprehensive staff training. We also provide 24/7 support during your first two weeks to ensure smooth operations.
Can clients book appointments online directly?
Yes! The system includes a customized online booking portal where clients can view availability, select services, choose time slots, and make advance payments. All bookings automatically sync with your studio calendar and send confirmation notifications.
How secure is our client data and photo storage?
We use enterprise-grade security with 256-bit encryption, secure cloud storage, and regular automated backups. All client data is stored in compliance with international privacy standards, and you maintain full control over access permissions and data retention policies.
Can we migrate data from our current booking system?
Absolutely! Our team provides free data migration services for all plans. We can import client information, booking history, payment records, and other relevant data from most popular systems including Excel spreadsheets, Google Calendar, and other studio management tools.
What kind of support and training do you provide?
All packages include comprehensive onboarding with personalized training sessions for your team. We provide 24/7 phone and chat support for the first 7 days, followed by business hours support (8am-6pm, except Sundays) for 3 months. Video tutorials and user guides are also available 24/7.
Can the system handle multiple studio locations?
Yes! The Enterprise plans support multi-location management. You can manage bookings, staff, equipment, and clients across multiple studio locations from a single dashboard, with location-specific reporting and analytics.
