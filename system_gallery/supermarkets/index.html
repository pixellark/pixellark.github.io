Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our supermarket management system

How long does it take to set up the system in our supermarket?
Most supermarkets are fully operational within 48 hours. Our team handles hardware installation, barcode setup, inventory data migration, and comprehensive staff training. We ensure zero disruption to your daily operations during the transition.
Can the system handle high-volume transactions during peak hours?
Absolutely! Our system is designed to handle thousands of transactions per hour across multiple counters. It includes queue management, rapid barcode scanning, and real-time inventory updates without any slowdown.
How does the inventory management prevent stock-outs and overstocking?
Our AI-powered system analyzes sales patterns, seasonal trends, and supplier lead times to automatically generate reorder alerts. It prevents stock-outs by forecasting demand and reduces overstocking through intelligent inventory optimization.
Is the system compatible with existing barcode scanners and hardware?
Yes! Our system integrates with most standard barcode scanners, receipt printers, cash drawers, and POS hardware. We provide compatibility assessment and can recommend cost-effective upgrades if needed.
What kind of support and training do you provide?
Every customizable package includes comprehensive on-site training for all staff, 24/7 support for the first 7 days, and ongoing chat support (8am-6pm, except Sundays) for 3 months. We ensure your team is confident and efficient with the new system.

Note: These benefits apply to customizable packages only, not template or subscription plans.
Can we track expiry dates and prevent selling expired products?
Yes! Our system automatically tracks expiry dates for all products and sends alerts before items expire. It can block sales of expired products at checkout and generate reports to help you implement effective product rotation strategies.
How secure is customer payment and business data?
Security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption, secure data backup, and role-based access controls. All payment processing follows international security standards, and your business data is protected with multiple backup systems.
