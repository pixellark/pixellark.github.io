Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our textile management system

How long does it take to implement the system in our business?
Most textile factories are fully operational within 48 hours. Our implementation team handles hardware setup, data migration, and comprehensive staff training. We also provide dedicated support during your first month to ensure smooth operations.
Can the system handle multiple production lines simultaneously?
Absolutely! Our system is designed to manage multiple production lines, different fabric types, and various garment categories simultaneously. You can track each line's performance, manage resources, and optimize production across your entire facility.
Does it integrate with existing accounting and ERP systems?
Yes! Our textile management system integrates seamlessly with popular accounting software and ERP systems used in Sri Lanka. We also provide custom API integrations for specialized systems used by larger textile manufacturers.
What kind of training do you provide for our staff?
We provide comprehensive training programs tailored to different roles - from floor operators to management. This includes on-site training sessions, video tutorials in Sinhala and Tamil, and ongoing support to ensure your team maximizes the system's potential.
Can we track fabric usage and minimize waste?
Our smart inventory module provides detailed fabric usage tracking, identifies waste patterns, and suggests optimization strategies. Many of our clients have reduced fabric waste by 40-50% within the first few months of implementation.
Is the system suitable for both garment manufacturing and textile production?
Yes! Our system is designed to handle both textile production (fabric manufacturing, dyeing, finishing) and garment manufacturing (cutting, sewing, finishing). We customize the modules based on your specific business requirements.
